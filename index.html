Cost savings
Staffing reduction — saves ~$130,000/year
No overnight front desk. No evening manager. Contract cleaning replaces in-house staff. Drops from ~4 FT roles to 1 part-time GM + 1 part-time food bar + cleaning contract.
Higher membership cap — 500 vs 350
24/7 spreads peak traffic across all hours. Off-peak 11pm–6am absorbs members who can't make it during rush. Every member above break-even is nearly pure margin.
Faster path to profitability
Lower fixed costs mean you need fewer members to turn profitable. Critical in months 6–12 when cash reserve is being drawn down.
Additional costs
Access control + security tech — $18,000 setup + $900/month
Biometric or keycard entry ($8k), camera system ($4k), smart lockers ($3k), member app + booking software ($3k setup). Monthly SaaS: ~$400–600/mo.
Higher utilities — +$6,000–8,000/year
HVAC, lighting, and security running 24/7. Partially offset by smart thermostats and programmable HVAC zones during off-peak hours (2am–5am).
Higher insurance premium — +$2,000–3,000/year
Unattended facility requires enhanced liability coverage. Some Ontario insurers require minimum camera coverage ratios and documented emergency response protocols.
Smart fridge + vending — $12,000 setup
Tap-to-pay smart fridges for pre-packaged meals and drinks. Protein shake vending. Generates revenue in off-hours without kitchen staff.
Operational requirements
Member vetting is non-negotiable
Boutique cap + 24/7 access = every member must complete ID verification, digital waivers, and an onboarding orientation session before solo access is granted. Consider a 2-week supervised trial period.
Ontario emergency protocol requirement
Unmanned facilities under Ontario OHSA require posted emergency procedures, accessible AED, and a defined response protocol. Work with your insurer and a lawyer on this before opening day.
Food bar is daytime-only for cooked meals
Ontario health code requires staff supervision for prepared food service. After-hours revenue comes from smart fridges (pre-packaged meals) and vending only.
